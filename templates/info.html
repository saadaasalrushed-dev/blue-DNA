Plastic Pollution

  • Over 8 million tons of plastic enter the ocean every year
  • Plastic takes 400-1000 years to decompose

Oil Spills

  • Oil spills can kill marine life instantly
  • Oil coats animals' feathers and fur
  • It takes decades for ecosystems to recover
  • One gallon of oil can contaminate 1 million gallons of water

  • Healthy oceans support billions of people
  • Beach cleanups can remove tons of trash
  • Every small action makes a difference
